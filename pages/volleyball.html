My Journey

I started playing beach volleyball about 4.5 years ago, which means I got a lot of catching up to do, to the people who have been doing this since they were kids. That’s also what makes this hunt fun. I love the chase: training hard, learning fast, and competing at the highest level in Norway.

I’m currently ranked Top 20 in Norway and around Top 800 in the world. This season on the Norwegian Tour I’ve picked up 3 silver medals and 8 bronze, and I placed 5th at the Norwegian Championship.

The results are becoming more consistent. I’ve had solid performances this year and enough proof to believe what I’m building is the right path forward. I’m still learning, still hungry, and volleyball somehow keeps getting more fun the better i become.

This is only the beginning and I'm curious what the next season holds for me.
